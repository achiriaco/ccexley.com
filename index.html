CC Exley is a Los Angeles-based screenwriter.

Experience includes work with Robin Armstrong’s Open Road Productions and Sunset Plaza-based New Media Artists. CC was a dedicated member of the Beyond Baroque Screenwriting Workshop (formerly Inscreenwriting) for many years and recently founded The Greenlight Group for intermediate-advanced feature film writers.

Current projects include a short film about Sybil Brand Institute for Women slated for production in 2017, and a feature-length script about a young woman’s plot for revenge.
For further information on current or upcoming projects, contact Ms. Exley at cc@ccexley.com.
